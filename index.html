Enneagram Cheat Sheet

⚖️ Type 1 – The Reformer

Core: Principled, purposeful, self-controlled, perfectionistic

Fear: Being corrupt or defective

Desire: To be good and have integrity

  • Wing 1w9 (The Idealist): More detached, calm, objective, philosophical
  • Wing 1w2 (The Advocate): More warm, helpful, critical of injustice, action-oriented

💝 Type 2 – The Helper

Core: Generous, demonstrative, people-pleasing, possessive

Fear: Being unloved or unwanted

Desire: To feel loved

  • Wing 2w1 (The Servant): More idealistic, principled, self-critical, controlled
  • Wing 2w3 (The Host/Hostess): More ambitious, image-conscious, charming, outgoing

🏆 Type 3 – The Achiever

Core: Adaptable, excelling, driven, image-conscious

Fear: Being worthless or without value

Desire: To feel valuable and worthwhile

  • Wing 3w2 (The Charmer): More sociable, encouraging, people-oriented, flattering
  • Wing 3w4 (The Professional): More introspective, emotionally aware, artistic, reserved

🎨 Type 4 – The Individualist

Core: Expressive, dramatic, self-absorbed, temperamental

Fear: Having no identity or personal significance

Desire: To find themselves and their significance

  • Wing 4w3 (The Aristocrat): More ambitious, polished, sociable, competitive
  • Wing 4w5 (The Bohemian): More withdrawn, intellectual, unconventional, private

🔍 Type 5 – The Investigator

Core: Perceptive, innovative, secretive, isolated

Fear: Being helpless, incapable, or overwhelmed

Desire: To be capable and competent

  • Wing 5w4 (The Iconoclast): More creative, subjective, emotionally intense, independent
  • Wing 5w6 (The Problem Solver): More analytical, loyal, cautious, collaborative

🛡️ Type 6 – The Loyalist

Core: Engaging, responsible, anxious, suspicious

Fear: Being without support or guidance

Desire: To have security and support

  • Wing 6w5 (The Defender): More introverted, analytical, independent, detached
  • Wing 6w7 (The Buddy): More outgoing, playful, distractible, reassurance-seeking

✨ Type 7 – The Enthusiast

Core: Spontaneous, versatile, distractible, scattered

Fear: Being deprived or trapped in pain

Desire: To be satisfied and fulfilled

  • Wing 7w6 (The Entertainer): More loyal, endearing, anxious, relationship-oriented
  • Wing 7w8 (The Realist): More assertive, pragmatic, competitive, intense

💪 Type 8 – The Challenger

Core: Self-confident, decisive, willful, confrontational

Fear: Being controlled or harmed by others

Desire: To protect themselves and control their fate

  • Wing 8w7 (The Maverick): More energetic, outgoing, impulsive, entrepreneurial
  • Wing 8w9 (The Bear): More calm, receptive, stubborn, quietly commanding

🕊️ Type 9 – The Peacemaker

Core: Receptive, reassuring, agreeable, complacent

Fear: Loss and separation

Desire: Inner peace and stability

  • Wing 9w8 (The Comfort Seeker): More assertive, grounded, stubborn, body-aware
  • Wing 9w1 (The Dreamer): More idealistic, orderly, detached, principled
